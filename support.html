Frequently Asked Questions
+
You can rent a car if you are above the age of 18.
+
You can rent a car through the “Hertz India” website or the mobile App.
+
All the cars are delivered and collected free of cost to the address specified at the time of making a booking.
Note: cars will not be delivered to any public places like malls, metro station, etc.
*Hertz holds the right to cancel the booking in such case.
+
As per the Hertz fuel policy:
  • The car will be delivered with a full tank and need to be returned in the same manner.
  • Customer pays for the fuel charges based on the usage.
  • If the car is returned with less than full tank fuel, Hertz will charge for the differential fuel.
  • The customer can either pay this directly by cash/card at the time of car pick up by Hertz driver or the same will be deducted from the security deposit.
  • The assessment made by Hertz on the differential fuel amount will be final.
+
Guest needs to inform Hertz in advance for rental extension. An extension or grace period of up to 1 hours is permissible if informed with no extra charges. If the extension exceeds 1 hours, following charges will be applicable for every hour including the 1-hour grace period.
Late Fee (Per Hour) Vehicle
Rs. 300/hr. Swift, Figo, i10 Grand, Dzire and Amaze
Rs. 400/hr. Ciaz, Etios, Scorpio, and City
Rs. 500/hr. Duster, Baleno, Brezza, Creta, Eco Sports, Ertiga, Innova, Innova Crysta and Xuv500
To avoid paying any extension fee we request our guest to extend the booking through the Hertz India website or mobile app. This is applicable on both self-drive and chauffeur-drive. In case there is any change in the plans i.e.: If your travel dates, or pick up/ drop location changes ahead of time, you can easily modify your already reserved rental with Hertz at no additional modification fee. Hertz provides you complete flexibility, ease and control over your car rental experience by letting you change your dates, city or even the car model.
+
Hertz Flex allows you to get a price benefit when you book for 7 days and above on select car models. Rentals start for as low as Rs 1250/day. To know more about Hertz Flex, click here. Note: Hertz Flex can be booked over the website only. We will soon be launching it on the “Hertz India” mobile app and mobile site.
